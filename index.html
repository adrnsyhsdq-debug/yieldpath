The Reality Check

The Freelance Income Rollercoaster

Three challenges every independent professional faces — and why traditional banking doesn't cut it.

Irregular Income

Dry months vs. feast months. One month you're flush, the next you're scraping by. Traditional salary-based budgeting tools simply don't understand the freelance rhythm.

Invoicing Nightmare

Clients paying late, manual chasing, and the awkward follow-up emails. Payment collection eats hours that should go toward billable work.

Tax & Compliance Confusion

Managing GBP/EUR/USD currencies, navigating VAT thresholds, and keeping tax buckets straight. Cross-border freelancing adds layers spreadsheets can't handle.
